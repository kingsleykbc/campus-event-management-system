Get Started

Project Overview

The On-Campus event management system is a web application that helps to manage all the events taking place in Babcock Universities. It Notifies the HOD of a student's Department and the School Vice Chancellor about any event being hosted in school.

Who Are You ?

ADMIN

This is the Part of the application that is controlled by the Vice Chancellor of Babcock University. It enables him to view all events in all the departments and their details / information. The VC can also approve or reject these events.

LOGIN

H.O.D

This is another Sub-system. It is semilar to that of the Admin Panel. However, the H.O.Ds can only view events going on in their departments, as well as approve or reject them.

LOGIN

STUDENTS

This is the part of the system that allows the uploading/posting of the events by students in various departments. It also enables students to view / print out clearance letters from the school if the event(s) have been approved by the Admin and H.O.D.
